About

Codie is an AI powered chatbot that is intended to help you with your Software Interview Preparation and doubts related to coding problems. Codie runs on the GPT-3 model created by OpenAI. Codie is currently available for free as a limited research preview. For any queries or suggestions, contact the creator: Vishruth

Supported Platforms

Currently, this extension works on the following platforms

  • Leetcode
  • Hackerrank
  • GeeksforGeeks
  • Interviewbit
  • AlgoExpert

We are working on adding support for more platforms. If you wish to suggest a platform, please do so here

Limitations

Since Codie runs on the GPT-3 model, it has inherent limitations. Codie tries its best to give you the most accurate and appropriate model, it may not do so always.
